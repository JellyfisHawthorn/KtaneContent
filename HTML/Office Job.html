Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes Mod Office Job

On the Subject of Office Job

This is what Employee 427 did every day of every month of every year.

When this module activates, it will display between one and three keyboard keys (“no” signs will appear above unused keys). Pressing and holding all displayed keys on your keyboard simultaneously will add six seconds (twenty on Twitch Plays) to the module’s timer; the light bar on the bottom will turn green to indicate this. Afterwards, release all held keys to reveal the next set of keys to press.

For each undisplayed alphanumeric key pressed, the timer will decrease by one second (unless on Twitch Plays) — the light bar will turn red when this happens.

The number of keys to press increases as you solve more modules.
