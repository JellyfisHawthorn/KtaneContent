Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes Mod Temperature Regulation

On the Subject of Temperature Regulation

So, you’re hot and you’re cold. You’re yes and you’re no. You’re in and you’re out, you blow up, a man down.

Note: If the band on the left side of the module is charging upward, you are looking at the wrong manual.

A “Temperature Regulation” module has a lever connected to a light, a speaker, and a thermometer scale.

The thermometer is directly connected to the bomb’s temperature. The higher the indicator is, the hotter the bomb gets.

Flicking the lever up will cause the module to heat up.

Flicking the lever down will cause the module to cool down.

Don’t let the module overheat or freeze, as both will short circuit the module’s insides and cost a strike.
